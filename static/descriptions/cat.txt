The cat is a carnivorous mammal known for its agility, grace, and independent nature. Cats are commonly kept as pets for their companionship and ability to control pests like rodents. They have sharp retractable claws and keen senses, making them efficient hunters. Cats can be found in various habitats worldwide, from urban environments to rural areas. They are known for their diverse personalities and behaviors, ranging from playful and affectionate to aloof and solitary.
